The Artist

Rafaela Oliveira is a Brazilian artist based in Toronto. Certified photographer by f/508 Photography School in 2016, but living a relationship with capturing moments since 2009. Over the past decade, her work is getting inspired by discomforts, blurry lines and boring moments.
